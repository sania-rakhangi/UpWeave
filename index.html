About Us

Feelgood Eco Nurture LLP, a Indore-based start-up, operates in Waste Management & Plastic Recycling. They specialize in innovative methods for reusing & recycling diverse post consumer waste. Currently, Feel Good is venturing into recycling and reusing ‘non-recyclable’ Multi-Layered Plastic (Grade 7 / Others). They have a patented technology for producing granules using mixed plastic waste and industrial waste from construction and metal industries. This technology enhances the strength of recycled granules. Their material, still in Research & Development, aims to manufacture Clothing ,Tiles, Paver Block, Roof Tiles & Bricks, and furniture. This initiative supports the Circular Economy and aims to create greater value.

Purpose

To create sustainable fashion while reducing plastic waste and promoting environmental responsibility.

Vision

To be a leading sustainable fashion brand that innovatively repurposes plastic waste.

Mission

To produce high-quality, stylish clothing from recycled PET bottles, contributing to a cleaner environment.
